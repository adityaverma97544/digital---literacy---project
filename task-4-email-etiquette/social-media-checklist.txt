Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist:

DO'S
1. Think before posting
2. Use strong passwords
3. Verify information
4. Respect others online
5. Use privacy settings

DON'TS
1. Do not share OTP/passwords
2. Do not post personal details
3. Do not click suspicious links
4. Do not spread fake news
5. Do not bully others
